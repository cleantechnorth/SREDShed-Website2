Bryan Watson has served as Managing Director of <a href="https://www.cleantechnorth.org/" target="_blank">CleanTech North</a> since 2012 and is a Founding Director of the <a href="https://octia.ca/" target="_blank">Ontario Clean Technology Industry Association</a>. He is also SVP at <a href="https://www.venbridge.com/" target="_blank">Venbridge</a> (Powered by <a href="https://www.easly.com/" target="_blank">Easly</a>). Widely regarded as a pillar of the Canadian cleantech sector, Bryan was honored with a <a href="https://clean50.com/honourees/bryan-watson/" target="_blank">2021 Clean50 Award</a> and a <a href="https://clean50.com/projects/" target="_blank">2025 Clean50 Top Project Award</a> for his leadership in forming <a href="https://www.cpacanada.ca/" target="_blank">CPA Canada's</a> Green Tech Working Group in 2023, facilitating key discussions with the <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency.html" target="_blank">CRA</a>, <a href="https://natural-resources.canada.ca/" target="_blank">Natural Resources Canada</a>, and the <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/department-finance.html" target="_blank">Department of Finance</a> on the Canadian Clean Economy Tax Credits.

Bryan has held leadership roles across Canada’s innovation and investment ecosystems, including Executive Director of <a href="https://www.nacocanada.com/" target="_blank">NACO Canada</a> and <a href="https://www.angelinvestorsontario.ca/" target="_blank">Angel Investors Ontario</a>. He has served on several boards, including <a href="https://www.enthusiastgaming.com/" target="_blank">Enthusiast Gaming</a> (<a href="https://www.tsx.com/" target="_blank">TSE: EGLX</a>), advised growth companies, and sits as an expert and reviewer for many federal and provincial organizations. He holds a Master’s from the <a href="https://www.st-andrews.ac.uk/" target="_blank">University of St. Andrews</a>, Scotland, where he was Canada’s sole <a href="https://www.chevening.org/" target="_blank">British Council Chevening Scholar</a>.
